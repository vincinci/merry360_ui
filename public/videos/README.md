# Hero Background Video

## Instructions
Place your hero background video here with the name: `hero-background.mp4`

## Recommended Specifications
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 3840x2160 (4K)
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds (will loop automatically)
- **File size**: Keep under 10MB for optimal loading
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9
- **Frame rate**: 30fps or 60fps

## Where to Get Free Videos
- [Pexels Videos](https://www.pexels.com/videos/)
- [Pixabay Videos](https://www.pixabay.com/videos/)
- [Coverr](https://coverr.co/)
- [Mixkit](https://mixkit.co/free-stock-video/)

## Current Setup
The video is set to:
- Autoplay on page load
- Loop continuously
- Muted (no audio)
- Cover entire hero section
- Fallback to CDN video if local file not found

## To Add Your Video
1. Download or create your video
2. Rename it to `hero-background.mp4`
3. Place it in this `/public/videos/` folder
4. Refresh your browser to see it

The app will automatically use your local video instead of the CDN fallback.
